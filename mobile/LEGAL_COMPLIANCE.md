## SEC Compliance Requirements

### 1. Avoid Prohibited Language

**NEVER USE** in app, marketing, or store listings:
- ❌ "Guaranteed returns"
- ❌ "Risk-free"
- ❌ "Can't lose"
- ❌ "Guaranteed profits"
- ❌ "Sure thing"
- ❌ "Get rich quick"
- ❌ "No risk"
- ❌ "Guaranteed winners"
- ❌ "100% success rate"
- ❌ "Foolproof strategy"

**USE INSTEAD**:
- ✅ "Educational information"
- ✅ "May help inform your research"
- ✅ "For informational purposes"
- ✅ "No guarantees"
- ✅ "Past performance does not indicate future results"
- ✅ "All investments carry risk"

### 2. Advertising Compliance (FINRA Rule 2210)

Even though we're not FINRA members, following these guidelines provides protection:

**Requirements**:
- No false or misleading statements
- No exaggerated claims
- No omission of material facts
- No predictions of performance
- Proper risk disclosure
- Clear distinction between facts and opinions
- No promissory language

### 3. Investment Advisers Act Compliance

**To Avoid Triggering Registration Requirements**:

✅ **DO**:
- Provide general educational content
- Offer broad market commentary
- Present multiple viewpoints
- Encourage users to do own research
- Recommend consulting licensed advisors

❌ **DON'T**:
- Give personalized investment advice
- Claim to manage portfolios
- Offer tailored recommendations
- Charge advisory fees
- Suggest specific buy/sell timing for individual users
- Create individualized investment plans

### 4. Anti-Fraud Provisions (Securities Act §17(a), Exchange Act §10(b))

**Avoid**:
- Material misrepresentations
- Omitting material facts
- Manipulative practices
- Deceptive statements

**Implement**:
- Accurate, truthful information
- Complete risk disclosure
- Clear source attribution
- Regular data updates
- Error correction procedures

## State Securities Laws (Blue Sky Laws)

## Insurance & Legal Protection

### Recommended Insurance Coverage:

1. **Errors & Omissions (E&O) Insurance**
   - Coverage: $1-2 million

   - Coverage: $1-5 million
   - Protects against: Data breaches, cyber attacks, privacy violations

3. **General Liability Insurance**
   - Coverage: $1 million
   - Protects against: General business liabilities

4. **Directors & Officers (D&O) Insurance**
   - Coverage: $1-3 million
   - Protects: Company leadership from personal liability

## App Store Specific Compliance

### Apple App Store Requirements:

**Financial Apps Section 4.5.4**:
- Must comply with all applicable law
- Must clearly disclose if not providing real trading
- Must include proper disclaimers
- Cannot mislead users about functionality

**Required Metadata**:
- Age rating: 17+ (Frequent/Intense Simulated Gambling)
- Content descriptor: Simulated Gambling
- Accurate app description

### Google Play Requirements:

**Financial Services Policy**:
- Clear disclosure of services provided
- Proper licensing information (or disclosure of lack thereof)
- Cannot facilitate illegal financial services
- Must comply with local laws

**Required**:
- Financial Services declaration in Console
- Accurate categorization
- Privacy policy link
- Terms of service link

## Red Flags to Avoid

**Activities That Could Trigger Regulatory Action**:

❌ Executing trades on behalf of users
❌ Holding user funds
❌ Offering margin or leverage
❌ Making specific buy/sell recommendations with timing
❌ Claiming to manage user portfolios
❌ Charging fees based on assets or performance
❌ Claiming to be an "investment advisor" without registration
❌ Guaranteeing returns or performance
❌ Using manipulative or deceptive practices
❌ Failing to disclose material risks
❌ Misrepresenting qualifications or track record
